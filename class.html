Marketing 305 marketing foundations

Professor: Sandi Luck

Marketing 305 is a business curriculm course that teaches students different marketing strategeis and the basics of the marketing world. Teaching students how to conduct market research out in the real world and the difference between primary and secondary data. Also teaches students the strategy of postioning products in a store and the company postioning statement.

Marketing 305 is held both through zoom and in the classroom. I am enrolled in the Monday Wednesday Friday class from 11:15-12:05. 80 students are enrolled in the course so in order to attend class you must sign up through black board. During class Professor Luck goes through powerpoints of the chapter and at the end of class puts us in groups for a group activity. Outside of class each week there is a quiz through stukent and a weekly assignment unless there is a exam that week.
